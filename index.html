Gukesh Dommaraju (born 29 May 2006), also known as Gukesh D, is an Indian and the reigning .
A chess prodigy, Gukesh is the youngest undisputed world champion, the youngest player to have surpassed a FIDE rating of 2750, doing so at the age of 17, and the third-youngest to have surpassed 2700 Elo at the age of 16. He earned the title of grandmaster at the age of 12 and is the third-youngest grandmaster in chess history.

Gukesh started playing chess at the age of 7. He won the under-12 title at the World Youth Chess Championship in 2018,
and multiple gold medals at the 2018 Asian Youth Chess Championship.
